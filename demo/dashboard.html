Creator dashboard

Accept payments through this Portal instance under your own address. Gates, Paylinks, and webhooks, all yours.

No username, no password. Sign in the same way anyone signs into Portal: send a zero-value shielded memo from your own wallet. That proof of wallet ownership is your account.

First time signing in from this wallet? We mint you a receiving address on the spot. It can take up to a minute.

How it identifies you: include your own Zcash address in the memo after the code. Type it in manually, or enable “include reply address” in Ywallet. Same address = same account, every time.

Webhook

Get an HMAC-signed POST the moment a payment into one of your Gates or Paylinks is detected, and again once confirmed. Must be a public http(s) URL; localhost and private addresses are rejected.

Verify each delivery with the x-portal-signature header: hex HMAC-SHA256 of the raw body, keyed with your webhook secret. See the docs for details.
