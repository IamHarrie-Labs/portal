Login

Log in with your wallet. Nothing else.

Scan a code with your wallet, approve a zero value shielded transaction, and you are signed in within seconds. No usernames, passwords, or personal information required.

The site only learns that you proved access. Everything else stays private.

Gates

Put a price on the door.

Turn any page, app, file, or community into paid access. Set a price once, and anyone who pays through their wallet gets in automatically, without you building your own payment system.

You control the price. If someone pays less than required, access stays locked until the full amount is received.

Paylinks

Get paid with a link.

Create a payment link in seconds. Share it anywhere, and anyone with a Zcash wallet can pay you directly. Every payment is verified on the Zcash mainnet as it arrives.

Use it for tips, invoices, donations, digital products, or a simple buy me a coffee page. The link never expires, many people can pay it, and a running count keeps score.
