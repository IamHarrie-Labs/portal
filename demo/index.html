Login

Sign in without an account.

Your wallet sends a zero value shielded transaction carrying a one time code. Portal watches it arrive on the Zcash mainnet, and you are in.

The site you signed into learns just one thing about you: that you showed up. Everything else stays yours.

1

A one time code appears on screen, as a QR or a tap to pay link.

2

Your wallet sends it back inside an encrypted memo, costing nothing beyond the network fee.

3

Portal sees it land on mainnet, usually within seconds, and you are signed in.

Gates

Put a price on the door.

A Gate is a login with a price. Set an amount and the same one step flow becomes a paywall, a membership, or a ticket booth, with nothing else for you to build and nobody standing between you and the payment.

The price lives on your server, so nobody can talk it down. If someone underpays, the door simply stays shut and tells them why.

Paylinks

Get paid with a link.

Choose an amount, add a label, and share the link. Anyone who opens it can pay you straight from their wallet, and Portal confirms each payment live on the mainnet.

It works as a tip jar, an invoice, or a buy me a coffee button. The link never expires, many people can pay it, and a running count keeps score.
